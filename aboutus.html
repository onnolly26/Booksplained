Who Are We?

We're an organisation created with the sole purpose of making literature more understandable and accessible for everyone.

We have curated and selected the best out of thousands of books to suit your needs and wants. We've done the rummaging so you don't have to!

Since starting from nothing we have come a long way while working with charities and sponsors to form partnerships in many different areas.

We do this to ensure our continual growth, expand our reach wherever we can and make it happen for anyone who loves books as much as we do.

We consistently aim to provide links to books at the cheapest possible prices and most suitable shipping based on your circumstances. That's all with the best discounts from trusted Australian retailers.

We also want to make the joy of reading available to all while making it easier for those who are disadvantaged, young learners, people living in remote areas and people with disabilities.
